The Process
How the 4 sessions unfold

Each session is designed to stand on its own — so you get value from day one, regardless of what comes next.

1
Group Session 1 · 2 hours · Zoom
Mapping reality together
Where are we now? The team maps the current situation, surfaces hidden assumptions, and builds a shared picture of what's actually happening — and what's getting in the way.
2
Group Session 2 · 2 hours · Zoom
Identifying the OBT and building the plan
What's the one change that creates the most leverage? The team defines a clear OBT, then uses the WOOP model to design a graduated action plan with real ownership.
3
1:1 Session 1 · 30 min · Zoom
Personal alignment
One-on-one with the manager: what's coming up, where does the plan need adjusting, and how to navigate personal resistance or external friction.
4
1:1 Session 2 · 30 min · Zoom
Follow-through and closing the loop
What's working? What still needs attention? Locking in the progress, consolidating learning, and leaving with a personal toolkit for sustaining the change independently.
